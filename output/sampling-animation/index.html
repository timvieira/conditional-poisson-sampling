Sampling from the Conditional Poisson Distribution

An animated walk through the product tree

Starting at the root with a quota of $n$ items, the algorithm walks the product tree top-down. At each internal node with quota $k$, it splits $k$ between left and right children with probability proportional to $P_L[j] \cdot P_R[k{-}j]$—the weighted Vandermonde identity. At the leaves, quota 1 = selected, quota 0 = excluded.
