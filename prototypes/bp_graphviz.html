Interactive backpropagation tree (Graphviz layout). The forward pass (left) computes the polynomial product tree bottom-up. The backward pass (right) propagates adjoints top-down. Each child's adjoint = cross-correlation of parent adjoint with sibling's forward polynomial. At the leaves, $\pi_i = w_i \cdot \bar{c}_1^{(i)}$. Drag the weight sliders to explore.
